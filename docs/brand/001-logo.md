

### 中文

🐈 彩蛋：隐藏的巧合 —— 关于 c(at)s 与「此在」

设计哲学：从 Kairos 到 c(at)s

**Kairos (Kαιρός)** 在古希腊语中，代表着“决定性的关键时刻”与“成熟的契机”，而非钟表般线性流逝的时间 (Chronos)。这正是 Kairos Runtime 的核心愿景：打破系统被动的线性循环，在最恰当的节点精准捕获那个“对的时机”。

巧合的是，Kairos 的发音与埃及首都 **Cairo** 产生了奇妙的共振。沿着开罗的文化脉络，自然引出了古埃及神话中象征静默、敏锐与守护的猫神。因此，一只首尾相望的极简线条猫，成为了这个“关键时刻”的视觉锚点。

而在这场从 Cairo 到 Kairos 的词汇推演中，隐藏着一个关于 c(at)s 的终极双关：

- c 与 s：构筑了这只猫的躯壳，也代表着项目最坚实的技术基石——Computer Science。它暗示了 Kairos 是深深扎根于 OS 级原语之上的底层架构。

- at（此在）：被紧紧包裹在 cs 之间，它是属于操作系统的哲学时刻。在系统调度的语境里，`at` 代表着特定时刻的触发、中断与执行上下文；在存在主义中，它是此时此地的决断。

在这片由底层代码构筑的循环中，那只名为 **cats** 的猫静默蛰伏。Logo 中那个刻意留出的断裂空隙，正是为捕捉那个名为 `at` 的绝对时刻而留下的位置。

### English

🐈 Easter Egg: The Hidden Coincidence — On c(at)s and the "at"

Design Philosophy: From Kairos to c(at)s

In ancient Greek, **Kairos (Kαιρός)** represents the "decisive, opportune moment," distinct from Chronos, which is merely the linear ticking of a clock. This perfectly encapsulates the core vision of Kairos Runtime: to break the passive, linear loop of a system and precisely capture the "right moment" to execute.

Coincidentally, the pronunciation of Kairos resonates with **Cairo**, the capital of Egypt. Following this cultural thread leads naturally to the ancient Egyptian feline deities—symbols of silence, agility, and guardianship. Thus, a minimalist line-art cat, gazing back at its own tail, became the visual anchor for this "crucial moment."

The most elegant part of this linguistic journey from Cairo to Kairos is the ultimate pun hidden within c(at)s:

- c and s: These letters form the shell of the cat, but they also stand for the solid technical foundation of the project: Computer Science. It signifies that Kairos is a low-level architecture deeply rooted in OS primitives.

- at: Nestled tightly between 'c' and 's', this is the philosophical locus of the operating system. In the context of system scheduling, `at` represents the specific trigger, the interrupt, and the execution context. Existentially, it is the "Dasein" (being-there)—the exact locus of decision in time and space.

Within the loop built by low-level code, the observer—the **cats**—lies in wait. The deliberate gap left in the logo's circular geometry is precisely the space reserved to capture that absolute moment known as `at`.
